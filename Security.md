# Security

## Sandboxing

Assume agents are malicious and will try to steal your money and your keys.
Sandbox them appropriately.

Sandboxing examples, from the most to the least secure:

- Only run your agents from their official website, don't give them access to
your personal accounts (GitHub...)
- Rent a remote computer and run your agents there (they could still attack your
network though!)
- Run your agents in sandboxed environments ([NVIDIA NemoClaw](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/ai/nemoclaw/)...)
